# Vo2max — Fifth Vital Sign

## TL;DR
VO2max measures the maximum rate at which your body can consume oxygen during exercise and is considered the single best predictor of long-term health and longevity. It is the result of all 5 vital signs — heart rate, blood pressure, respiratory rate, temperature, and oxygen saturation — working together at peak efficiency.

## Key Concepts
- VO2max as master metric for longevity and healthspan
- VO2max is the integration of all 5 vital signs under load
- Higher VO2max = lower all-cause mortality risk
- Trainable at any age through aerobic and interval training
- Declines ~1% per year after 25 without training intervention
